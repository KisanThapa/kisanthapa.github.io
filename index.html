PhD Candidate in Computer Science

I am a PhD candidate at the University of Massachusetts Boston, specializing in Computational Biology and Deep Learning. My research focuses on developing and applying advanced computational methods, particularly leveraging deep learning and graph neural networks (GNNs), to decipher complex biological systems from omics data.

I work within the Network Biology Laboratory under the supervision of Prof. Özgün Babur. My passion lies in bridging the gap between cutting-edge AI techniques and biological discovery, creating intuitive software tools that empower researchers.
